Stochastic Simulation Features

This game uses Monte Carlo methods and geographic modeling:

  • 🪿 Latitude-based survival probabilities (like real goose migration!)
  • 🗺️ Directional migration changes your location and climate zone
  • 💕 Breeding success depends on energy levels and random chance
  • 🎯 Predator attacks use distance-based probability calculations
  • ⚡ Energy loss during migration with weather variance
  • ⛈️ Random environmental events (storms) can strike anywhere

How to Play

  • 🐥 Eggs hatch into goslings. Goslings grow into adult geese.
  • 🐣 Use Hatch button to lay eggs.
  • 🦊 Use Hide button to avoid predators , fox and eagles.
  • 🔊 HONK at predators to try to scare them away.
  • 🧭 Migrate when vegetation gets sparse with short or long fligts.
  • ⚡ Long flights, storms or bad habitats may affect Health. Low health affects survival rates.
  • 🌡️ Climate zones affect survival rates. Migrate to a different climate if needed.
    • ☀️ Temperate: Ideal habitat for geese, best survival chances
    • 🌨️ Subarctic/Subtropical: Satisfactory climate for geese
    • 🌴 Tropical: Harsher conditions, temperature too high and lacks water
    • ❄️ Arctic / Antarctic: Extreme conditions, unfavorable for survival.
