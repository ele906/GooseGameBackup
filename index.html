Stochastic Simulation Features

This game uses Monte Carlo methods and geographic modeling:

  • 🪿 Latitude-based survival probabilities (like real goose migration!)
  • 🗺️ Directional migration changes your location and climate zone
  • 💕 Breeding success depends on energy levels and random chance
  • 🎯 Predator attacks use distance-based probability calculations
  • ⚡ Energy loss during migration with weather variance
  • ⛈️ Random environmental events (storms) can strike anywhere

How to Play

  • 🌡️ Climate zones are crucial for survival. Harsher climates have lower survival rates:
    • ☀️ Temperate (30-50°N): Best survival rates
    • 🌨️ Subarctic/Subtropical: Good climate for geese
    • 🌴 Tropical: Harsh conditions
    • 🧊 Arctic / Antarctic: Extreme conditions
  • 🐥 Eggs hatch into goslings
  • 🪿 Goslings grow into adult geese
  • 🦊 Avoid foxes and eagles - predators want to eat you!
  • 🔊 HONK at predators to try to scare them away.
  • 🌳 Click individual geese or use "Hide" button to avoid predators
  • 🐣 Use Hatch button to lay eggs. Hatching geese can't move.
  • 🧭 Migrate with short or long flights to move to a better habitat.
  • ⚡ Energy affects survival. Long flights deplete a lot of energy.
  • ⛈️ Random storms can hit and affect survival
